MEDICDIARY
One place for all your health records.

Providing a hassle-free environment to manage your health records.

We at MedicDiary constantly endeavour to make management of your health records simple and easy.
Along with health record management we have a bunch of other features that will help you know everything you need to know about common diseases.

Features of our project

  • Upload your lab test results
  • Keep a track of your past and current medication
  • Enhanced security
  • Get more informatin on the symptoms and causes of any disease
